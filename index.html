Our mission

More than
a thrift store

Homeboy Threads is the retail arm of Homeboy Industries — the world's largest gang intervention program. When you buy here, you're funding job training, mental health support, and real second chances for people who need it most.

The model

How it works

01

Brands donate

Premium brands like Reformation and Guess donate overstock, samples, and returned items to us instead of sending them to landfill. Every piece is inspected and graded before it hits our store.

02

You shop

Browse curated pieces from real brands at 50–70% below retail. Each listing includes the brand, condition grade, measurements, and a direct line to the impact your purchase creates.

03

We fund futures

Every dollar of revenue goes straight back into Homeboy Industries programming — job training, mental health services, tattoo removal, and reentry support for men and women in Los Angeles.
